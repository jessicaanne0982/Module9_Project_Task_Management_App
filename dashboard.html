Types of Tasks Available

Manage and schedule daily, weekly, and monthly household cleaning tasks, including vacuuming, dusting, scrubbing, wiping surfaces, and maintaining cleanliness in all areas of the home.

Plan meals ahead of time to save time and reduce stress. Create weekly or monthly meal plans, generate grocery lists based on recipes, and track ingredients. Manage cooking schedules, recipes, and special dietary preferences for efficient, healthy meals.

Organize grocery lists and track supplies to ensure you always have what you need. Create shopping lists for routine groceries, stock up on essentials, and avoid duplicate purchases.

Stay on top of routine home maintenance tasks, from changing HVAC filters to checking smoke detectors. Set reminders for seasonal tasks and track ongoing repairs.

Organize and declutter rooms, closets, and storage spaces. Create personalized checklists to manage decluttering projects, categorize belongings, and donate or discard unused items.

Manage tasks related to pet care, including feeding schedules, grooming, vet appointments, and pet supplies. Track important dates like vaccinations and flea treatments, and ensure your pets stay happy and healthy with proper care.

Track household expenses, manage bills, and create a family budget. Monitor utility costs, subscriptions, and savings goals, while staying on top of important financial deadlines.

Digitally store and organize household documents such as warranties, insurance policies, tax information, and appliance manuals. Keep important records secure and easily accessible, ensuring you never lose track of crucial information.
